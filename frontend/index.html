📖 How to Use This Tool

Step 1: Select Study Area

Choose from predefined areas in Kenya or use the current extent.

Step 2: Choose Weighting Scheme

• Balanced: Equal importance to all factors
• Hydrogeology Focus: Geology and slope prioritized
• Climate Focus: Rainfall and landuse prioritized

Step 3: Select Season

Adjusts rainfall impact based on seasonal variations.

Step 4: Run Analysis

Click "Run Analysis" and wait 10-30 seconds for processing.

Step 5: Interpret Results

• Red: Very Low Potential
• Orange: Low Potential
• Yellow: Moderate Potential
• Light Green: High Potential
• Dark Green: Very High Potential

About AHP: Analytical Hierarchy Process combines multiple factors (geology, slope, rainfall, landuse) with expert-derived weights to identify groundwater potential zones.
